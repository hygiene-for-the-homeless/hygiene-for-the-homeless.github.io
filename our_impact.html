Media Coverage.

The media impact created.

Hygiene for the Homeless Canada and our homeless related events have been covered by local,national and international media, which have raised more awareness about homeless problem in Canada and beyond. Among those media are: CBC Radio (French), Omni TV, Fairchild TV, BCIT News, Xinhua News Agency, Sing Tao Daily, Ming Pao, Surrey Now-Leader and China News Agency. St George's School eNews also published a story about our Finding Home for the Homeless conference.
